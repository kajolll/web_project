The idea for this project was born after our visit to a government
school in Kolar district of Karnataka. The yound minds there were
so talented and artistic, and so tender and humble at the same time.
It brought upon the realization that, there is so much joy in giving to
others that don't have what we do.

This platform was therefore, created to help you experience the same joy we did.

The More The Merrier.

We have collaborated with a few government schools in Karnataka,
to help the students studying there in any way possible. We conduct
events and activites for the students once a month. All you have to do
to be a part is register to volunteer in any of these events!

Together we can make this a memorable experience both for the students and you.
