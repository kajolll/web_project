About Us

Our organisation believes in education for all. We aim at connecting young educated people with underprivilged children in government schools across Karnataka. It was started by Harita A and Kajol R Singh after a visit to an Ngo in the rural areas of Karnataka. We believe that there are many more govenment schools like this where children dont get all the benifits as the children studying in private schools in citiesas they would provide better infrastructure, better teacher to student ratio, have a clean & hygienic facility, provide better environmentfor students with options of personality development and extracurricular activities. We want to hel[p children in govenment schools to get the same privileges.

This will be a great opportunity for those who think like us. Help the underprivileged children in their overall development. Our organisation was started in a hope to help those children who have interest in academics but are not that privileged to do so due to proverty in the places they belong to. We hope to provide the platform for young educated people in cities to help children in rural areas.

OUR MISSION  
To help children in rural areas be privileged enough to go behind their dreams and achieve them. To connect young aspirants from cities with children who need thir help for over all development.
